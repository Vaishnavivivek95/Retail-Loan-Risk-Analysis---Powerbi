| Attribute Name | Description | Data Type | Business Relevance |
| :------------- | :---------- | :-------- | :----------------- |
| Applicant ID | Unique identifier assigned to each loan applicant | Numeric / Text | Used to uniquely track and reference each application |
| Age | Age of the applicant in completed years | Numeric | Important for eligibility checks and risk profiling |
| Gender | Gender of the applicant | Categorical | Used for demographic and portfolio analysis |
| Marital Status | Marital status of the applicant | Categorical | Indicates financial responsibilities and stability |
| Annual Income | Total annual income of the applicant | Numeric (Currency) | Core factor for affordability and repayment capacity assessment |
| Loan Amount | Total loan amount applied for by the applicant | Numeric (Currency) | Determines exposure level and loan risk |
| Credit Score | Credit score representing applicant's creditworthiness | Numeric | Primary indicator of default risk |
| No of Dependent | Number of dependents financially supported by the applicant | Numeric | Higher dependents may reduce disposable income |
| Existing Loan | Indicates whether the applicant has existing loan obligations | Categorical | Used to assess current debt burden |
| Employment Status | Current employment status of the applicant | Categorical | Reflects income stability |
| Loan Approved | System or bank decision on loan application | Categorical | Original decision field for approval analysis |
| Age Category | Age group derived from applicant age | Categorical | Helps segment applicants by life stage and risk |
| Income Category | Income slab derived from annual income | Categorical | Simplifies affordability and eligibility analysis |
| Loan Approval Status | Standardized approval outcome for reporting | Categorical | Key outcome variable for dashboards and KPIs |
| Loan to Value Ratio | Ratio of loan amount to collateral or asset value | Numeric (Percentage) | Measures collateral risk and lending exposure |
| LTV Category | Risk category derived from Loan to Value Ratio | Categorical | Enables quick identification of high-risk loans |
| Credit Band | Creditworthiness category derived from credit score | Categorical | Used to segment customers by credit quality |
| Employment Type | Classified employment type derived from employment status | Categorical | Supports stability and risk-based analysis |
| Dependency | Categorized dependency level based on number of dependents | Categorical | Assists in repayment capacity evaluation |
| Loan to Credit Ratio | Ratio of loan amount to credit score | Numeric | Measures loan exposure relative to credit strength |
| LTC Category | Risk category derived from Loan to Credit Ratio | Categorical | Helps identify credit over-leveraging |
| Credit Age Ratio | Ratio of credit history length to applicant age | Numeric | Reflects maturity of credit behavior |
| Credit Age Category | Credit maturity category derived from credit age ratio | Categorical | Distinguishes new borrowers from experienced ones |
